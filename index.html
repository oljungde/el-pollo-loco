El Pollo Loco

All the chickens have broken out and gone mad. If our Mexican friend Pepe doesn't do something, they will eat all the seeds and destroy the harvest. Help Pepe defeat the crazy chickens. But be careful, the chickens will fight back and in the end the big rooster is waiting and he is mighty angry.

To move Pepe, use the arrow keys to go left or right.

To defeat a chick or a chicken you have to jump on it, use the arrow up key.

To defeat the big rooster you have to throw the salsa bottles, you can collect, at him, use the "d" key, on a mobile device use the right key in the bottom.

Good luck, you will need it.
